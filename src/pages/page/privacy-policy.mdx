# Privacy Policy  
**FoxTrot Aviation Services, LLC**  
**Effective Date: January 1, 2025**

FoxTrot Aviation Services, LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, interact with our services, or communicate with us via telephone, email, or other means. By using our website or providing your information, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

- **Personal Information**: Name, phone number, email address, mailing address, and other contact details.
- **Non-Personal Information**: IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and usage details.
- **Communication Data**: Records of emails, phone calls, or messages exchanged with us.
- **Cookies & Tracking Technologies**: We use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance user experience.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

- To provide and improve our services.
- To communicate with you about your inquiries or transactions.
- To send marketing and promotional messages (with consent where required by law).
- To comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies.

---

## 3. Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) Compliance

In compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA):

- **Consent for Calls & Text Messages**: By providing your phone number, you consent to receiving calls and text messages from us, including marketing and informational messages. Message and data rates may apply.
- **Opt-Out Option**: You can opt out of marketing messages at any time by replying STOP to any message or contacting us directly.
- **No Third-Party Sales**: We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

---

## 4. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information with:

- **Service Providers**: Third parties that assist us in providing services.
- **Legal Authorities**: When required by law or to protect our rights.
- **Business Transfers**: In case of a merger, sale, or acquisition.

---

## 5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 6. Your Rights & Choices

- **Access & Correction**: You may request access to your personal data and request corrections.
- **Opt-Out**: You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.
- **Cookies Management**: You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

---

## 7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on our website with the updated effective date.
